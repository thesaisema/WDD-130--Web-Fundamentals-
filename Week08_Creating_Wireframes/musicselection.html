M U S I C FTS

Ever heard of "Music for the Soul"? Here are some phenomenal albums by Erykah Badu,
Tems, Victoria Monet, and Alina Baraz, to be able to relax your soul, mind, and heart. It is important
to take a break from whatever is stressing you out, so why not spend some time listening to some music.

The album titled, "Mama's Gun" by Erykah Badu features some classic hits like "Didn't cha Know" and "In Love with You".
If you want to listen to an album with soft hits like "Higher" or "Free Mind", "For Broken Ears" by Tems is the album for you.
Victoria Monet's "Jaguar" album has a free spirited kind of vibe, regarding the songs "We Might Be Falling In Love" and "Jaguar".
"The Colors of you" is an album by Alina Baraz, that has a mysterious yet a soft vibe to it when listening to the songs "I Don't Even Know Why Though" and "Tainted".
